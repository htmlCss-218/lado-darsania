Formula Drift Overview

Formula Drift is a motorsport series that is the premier championship for professional drift racing in the United States. The sport involves drivers intentionally oversteering their cars in a controlled manner, often sliding sideways around a circuit. The competition is judged based on speed, angle, and style, with points awarded for technique and precision.

Formula Drift events take place at various racetracks and stadiums across the U.S., where drivers compete in a bracket-style competition. Fans pack the stands to witness incredible driving feats and cheer for their favorite drivers as they battle for the championship title.
